# PlayStation Project Plan: Days of the Apocalypse

## Working Title
**Days of the Apocalypse**

---

## Target Platform(s)
- PlayStation 5 (Primary)
- PlayStation 4 (Secondary Support)
- PlayStation VR2 (Future Consideration)

---

## Appeal: Gameplay Experience & Genre

### Primary Genre
**Action-Adventure / Survival**

### Gameplay Experience
Days of the Apocalypse delivers an immersive post-apocalyptic survival experience combining:
- **Intense Action Sequences**: Fast-paced combat with dynamic enemy encounters
- **Strategic Resource Management**: Scavenging, crafting, and inventory management
- **Environmental Storytelling**: Discover the world's mysteries through exploration
- **Choice-Driven Narrative**: Player decisions shape the story and world state
- **Cooperative Elements**: Optional multiplayer survival modes

### Core Appeal
Players seek escapism and empowerment—the fantasy of surviving against impossible odds. Days of the Apocalypse delivers visceral, adrenaline-pumping gameplay within a richly detailed, deteriorating world.

## Scope: Key Features, Design & Mechanics

### Core Gameplay Systems

#### 1. **Exploration & Navigation**
- Expansive open-world map (60+ km²) with vertical traversal
- Dynamic weather system affecting visibility and enemy behavior
- Underground bunker networks and vertical city ruins
- Fast-travel limited to discovered safe zones

#### 2. **Combat System**
- Hybrid real-time combat with tactical pause elements
- Melee, ranged, and improvised weapons with durability
- Enemy AI adapts to player tactics and strategy
- Difficulty scaling without traditional difficulty modes

#### 3. **Survival Mechanics**
- Resource management: food, water, medicine, ammunition
- Health, hunger, thirst, and fatigue systems
- Environmental hazards: radiation, toxic zones, extreme temperatures
- Crafting system for weapons, gear, and consumables (150+ recipes)

#### 4. **Narrative & Choice**
- 30+ hours of single-player story content
- Branch narrative with 5 major story endings
- Dynamic dialogue system (1,000+ dialogue trees)
- Companion loyalty and relationship system

#### 5. **Progression Systems**
- XP-based skill trees (Combat, Survival, Technical)
- Equipment modification and upgrades
- Base building and customization
- Character statistics and perks

#### 6. **Multiplayer Features** (Post-Launch)
- 2-4 player co-op campaign (Campaign+)
- Asynchronous PvP arena mode (Wasteland Clash)
- Seasonal pass with cosmetics and battle pass
- Cross-generation PS4/PS5 compatibility

### Technical Features
- **DualSense Integration**: Haptic feedback for weapon feel, adaptive triggers for weapon resistance
- **Activity Cards**: Quick-launch into story chapters, multiplayer modes
- **Save State Cloud**: Cross-console save progression
- **Performance Modes**: Quality (4K/30fps) and Performance (1440p/60fps)

## Theme: Proposed Look & Feel

### Visual Aesthetic
**"Decay with Beauty"** — Nature reclaiming civilization

- **Color Palette**: Desaturated earth tones with neon accent colors (survivor base lighting)
- **Architecture**: Brutalist concrete structures overgrown with vegetation
- **Character Design**: Worn, practical clothing; scars and weathering visible on survivors
- **Enemy Design**: Horrific but grounded mutations; biological horror rather than supernatural

### Atmosphere
- **Tone**: Grim but not hopeless; moments of beauty amid desolation
- **Audio Design**: 
  - Dynamic ambient soundscape (wind, distant machinery, wildlife)
  - Diegetic music (survivor camps, radio broadcasts)
  - Tension-building orchestral score during combat
- **Environmental Design**: Abandoned cities, overgrown highways, underground systems, survivor settlements

### Influences
- *The Last of Us Part II* (atmosphere, character depth)
- *Fallout: New Vegas* (choice-driven narrative)
- *Horizon Zero Dawn* (post-apocalyptic beauty)
- *S.T.A.L.K.E.R.* series (survival immersion)
